## 别放弃希望

![别放弃希望](../../image/%E4%B8%93%E8%BE%91/1995-03-27_%E8%BF%98%E6%98%AF%E6%9C%8B%E5%8F%8B/%E5%88%AB%E6%94%BE%E5%BC%83%E5%B8%8C%E6%9C%9B.jpg)
